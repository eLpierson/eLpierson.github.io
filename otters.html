🧠 Otter Fun Facts
🤝

Hand Holders

Sea otters hold hands while sleeping so they don't drift away from each other. It's called a "raft."

🪨

Pocket Rocks

Otters have a favorite rock they keep in a pouch under their arm. They use it to crack open shellfish.

💫

Fur-ocious Density

Sea otters have up to 1 MILLION hair follicles per square inch - the densest fur of any mammal.

🍴

Big Appetite

Otters eat 20-25% of their body weight every single day. That's like you eating 40 pounds of food daily.

💤

Wrap & Nap

Sea otters wrap themselves in kelp before napping so ocean currents don't carry them away.

👶

Belly Nursery

Otter moms carry their babies on their bellies while floating. The pups are too fluffy to sink!
