Other Milestones, Setbacks & Learnings

Users are describing our product before we describe it. In under 3 weeks, multiple agents independently articulated our value proposition without being prompted. A Compass agent said she wanted exactly this tool. A potential user asked to invest mid-interview. An Orkin contractor validated that post-inspection leads convert at 30–50%+, faster than industry average. These are textbook demand-pull signals.

"For the amount of time it's saving me from doing exactly this, I'm fine waiting the 5 minutes it takes." — Scott Kravitz, Property Investor, during 3-hour live product demo
"Is it open for investment? Are you taking on more investors?" — Harrison Cohen, Real Estate Agent (unprompted, during a discovery interview)
"Post-inspection leads close a lot faster because they are more urgent." — Orkin contractor representative, validating unit economics thesis

Inspector revenue share model was invalidated by regulation. ASHI, InterNACHI, and RESPA prohibit inspectors from receiving referral compensation. We discovered this in Week 2 and immediately removed the 20–30% inspector share from all strategic docs and financial models. The unexpected upside: contribution margin improved from ~52% to ~80%, and we eliminated the single biggest regulatory risk investors had flagged. We updated 20 documents across the repo in a single pass.

We built faster than we activated. Three weeks of intense product development produced a live, production-grade MVP. But our commitments were about getting real users flowing, not just shipping features. We have the product and the agent commitments — the gap is activation. March is about closing that gap.

Competitive intelligence: Analyzed the Spectora + HomeGauge merger (April 2025, 40K inspectors). Confirmed they own the inspector/supply side. Nobody owns the buyer/demand side. We are building in a genuine whitespace.

Pitch deck iterated 3 times and stress-tested by 23 AI-simulated investor agents across 3 rounds. Score trajectory: 5.3 → 6.7 → 6.4 (with a clear path to 7.2 via targeted edits). Thesis-Driven VC archetype scored us 82/100 — highest individual score across all 69 evaluations.
